**APPENDIX A-01**

**Roles & Responsibilities**

|                     |            |                  |            |
|---------------------|------------|------------------|------------|
| **Document ID:**    | A-01       | **Version:**     | 1.0        |

1\. Quality Manager

**Responsibilities:**

- Final approval of all SOPs and quality documentation

- Approval of production deployments

- Approval of lab results and IQ/OQ/PQ protocols

- Management of CAPA and deviation processes

2\. Development Lead

**Responsibilities:**

- Technical leadership and architecture decisions

- Code review and Pull Request approvals

- Merge authority for main branch

- Change Request creation and technical assessment

3\. QA Lead

**Responsibilities:**

- Independent quality assurance and testing

- Approval of Change Requests

- Lab procedures execution (IQ/OQ/PQ)

- Final approval of lab results

4\. SRE/DevOps Lead

**Responsibilities:**

- Infrastructure and deployment management

- Production deployment execution

- Merge authority for main branch

- Lab equipment setup and validation support

5\. Product Owner

**Responsibilities:**

- Product requirements and prioritization

- Change Request initiation

- Stakeholder communication (no approval authority)
